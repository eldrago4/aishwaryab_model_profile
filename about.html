Aishwarya B is a model and an architect with Konkani roots. She was born and raised in Mumbai and is now based in Delhi, India. She is a trained Kathak dancer and a culture enthusiast. She uses different mediums of arts to express herself. This brings out her versatility in modelling as well. She easily transitions between high fashion editorials and commercial campaigns with a portfolio that includes names such as Vogue, Byredo and Amazon.
